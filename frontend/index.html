About FarmaSathi

FarmaSathi bridges the gap between farmers and agricultural experts using AI technology. Our platform provides timely, region-specific advice to help farmers make informed decisions.

We understand that farmers face critical challenges related to pests, weather, inputs, and market trends. Our mission is to make expert advice accessible to every farmer, regardless of location or literacy level.
